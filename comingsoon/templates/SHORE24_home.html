ABOUT SHORE'24

Welcome to SHORE Fest - GITAM University's Annual Fest
An otherworldly experience with a kaleidoscope of cultural delicacies laced with music, art, food, sports and everything in between. With a rich history from years' worth of people before us, SHORE 2024 is a hot pot of art and athleticism. The beautiful yet hustling shores of Vizag inspire and embody the spirit of Shore, just as the calming briny breezes and the crashing waves illustrate the youthful energy of Gitamites.


2nd, 3rd & 4th FEBRUARY, 2024

THEME FOR SHORE'24


Inclusivity, equality and health are few of the most essential aspects of one's life. Team SHORE will be working in 2024 with these in mind, aiming to promote GENDER EQUALITY, encourage healthy lifestyles and enhanced well being. The unfair gap between the different genders regarding opportunities, rights, and representation has been a focal point of our initiatives along with the declining health of our generation. We hope to bring about awareness and provide information regarding these issues, in our attempt to encourage people towards a society that is inclusive and healthy both on the inside and outside.

EVENTS

CULTURALS
Experience a cultural extravaganza like never before at SHORE Fest 2024! Dive into a kaleidoscope of traditions, dances, and art forms that reflect the diversity of our vibrant heritage. From mesmerizing folk performances to breathtaking art exhibitions, immerse yourself in a celebration of cultures from around the globe. Join us to witness the fusion of creativity and tradition that promises to leave you spellbound.
SPORTS
Get ready to unleash your competitive spirit at SHORE Fest 2024! Whether you're a seasoned athlete or a sports enthusiast, there's something for everyone. Feel the adrenaline rush as you participate in thrilling sporting events, from intense football matches to heart-pounding relay races. It's time to showcase your skills, teamwork, and sportsmanship on the vibrant shores of Vizag. Join us for an unforgettable experience filled with excitement and camaraderie!
TECHNICAL
Calling all innovators and tech enthusiasts! SHORE Fest 2024 brings you a platform to showcase your ingenuity and creativity. Engage in cutting-edge workshops, mind-boggling hackathons, and stimulating technical competitions. Explore the latest advancements in technology and embrace the opportunity to collaborate, learn, and innovate. Join us on this journey of discovery and innovation as we push the boundaries of what's possible in the world of technology.

Frequently Asked Questions

The 2nd Edition of SHORE Fest will be organized from 2nd to 4th FEBRUARY 2024.

The event registration process will begin on 20th January and will be open throughout the fest, except from 6 PM to 11 PM during concert nights. Entry to the concert nights will be permitted only after registration is completed.

Transportation services will not be provided by the SHORe Committee or the University.

You can register at the SHORe website or through the QR codes that are available all over the GITAM VISAKHAPATNAM Campus . For further assistance, registration desks are located throughout the campus where you can Self register or our team can help you out.

The celebs performing at the SHORe Fest 2024 concert nights will be announced soon on the SHORe Fest, GITAM Visakhapatnam Instagram page

No, refund will not be given if you change your mind after purchasing the ticket.

SHORE Fest has a huge diversity of events and various other elements planned for the attendees, starting with concert night to various stalls and games. While you walk through the streets of GITAM during Fest, keep a good view of your surroundings or you could miss out on something interesting.

You will be receiving a mail which contains the soft copy of your ticket attached to it, while entering the concert night you have to carry an identity card along with your digital ticket.
